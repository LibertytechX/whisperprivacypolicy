WHISPER BACKUP & RESTORE PRIVACY POLICY

Whisper BackUp & Restore is a tool to help you back up your text messages and call log information to an email account. To function properly, it needs to have full access to various types of private data stored on your phone as well as your email account.

Your data is accessed and then transferred to a place of your choice, usually your Gmail account. Whisper BackUp & Restore does not store private data on your phone nor does it send it to any other service except the one defined by you.

Your usage data is not shared with anybody, there are no ads, analytics or other type of tracking code embedded in the application.

Access to personal information

The following personally identifiable information is accessed by Whisper BackUp & Restore:

         full name

         mobile phone number

         email address

         other emails

         contacts (address book)

         text messages

         call logs

         calendar data

The access takes place with the sole purpose of transmitting and storing selected information to provide you with a convenient backup of your data. If you choose to store your text messages in Gmail, the resulting storage and processing is governed by the Google Privacy Policy, see there for details.

How we protect your Personal Information

To help protect the privacy of data and personal information we collect and hold, we maintain physical, technical and administrative safeguards. We update and test our security technology on an ongoing basis.

We train our employees about the importance of confidentiality and maintaining the privacy and security of your information. Access to your Personal Information is restricted to employees who need it to provide benefits or services to you.

Access to Google Drive

Our apps can be configured to automatically upload the backups to your Google Drive account. To facilitate this, Whisper BackUp & Restore will request you to allow it to access your Google Drive account on your behalf.

Whisper BackUp & Restore only accesses the xml backup files in the configured folders and only uploads files when a backup is performed (automatic or manual).

It can read the files when you View a file within the app or when you restore from a backup.

It can delete the files if it is configured to automatically delete older backups or if you manually delete backups from within the app.

It does NOT access the files at any other time and never sends the contents of the backups or any other data about the files on Google Drive to the developers or any other third-party.

Access to Gmail

Whisper BackUp & Restore can be configured to automatically email the backups using your Gmail account. To facilitate this, the app will request you to allow it to access your Gmail account on your behalf.

Whisper BackUp & Restore only uses the access to Gmail to send emails on your behalf and does not use this access for anything else.

If you do not want to store your data in Gmail you have also the option to have your text messages uploaded to an IMAP server under your control; see the Advanced configuration option for more.

Revoking access

To revoke Whisper BackUp & Restore access to your data simply delete it and make sure it is no longer linked to your account. Visit Apps connected to your account for details.

In-App Advertising

Whisper BackUp & Restore may display advertisement banners within the apps. Various third-party services are used for displaying these ads. No personally identifiable information is shared with the advertising service providers and you can opt-out of the advertisements from the Settings screen of the apps.

Transactional information

If you decide to make a donation from within the app the following information is automatically transmitted along with the purchase order:

         post code

         city

         country

The in-app purchase is handled by Google. Please refer to the Google Payments Privacy Notice for more information about how payment information is processed and stored.
